External Resources

Mood.gg: Music player with customised playlists for each champion.
Op.gg: A player analysis tool with access to ongoing games.
Probuilds: A database of games played by professional players. Can search by champion and view build path/runes/etc. for individual games.
Runeforge: A database of rune pages for each champion, along with descriptions of why particular runes are chosen.
