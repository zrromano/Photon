Slayer Calculator

Game: Runescape
Link
Enter the number of slayer points you want to earn, which slayer master you're using, and your current task streak and this tool will tell you how many slayer tasks you need to complete to reach your goal.

Unfortunately, the calculator doesn't support chaning slayer masters between tasks and it doesn't factor in the chance of recieving a special assignment.

NPC Generator

Game: Various tabletop RPGs
Link
Generates a basic outline for an RPG character. Stats generated, but are not limited to: Name, race, age, ability scores, appearance, and personality quirks.

The generator was written with the Pathfinder RPG system in mind, but characters are general enough to easily convert to other systems. The only rules taken specifically from Pathfinder are those for ability scores and aging's effects on them.
